About the Laboratory

The UNILAG Concrete Laboratory provides facilities for concrete mix design, cube testing, and documentation for both professional practice and academic research.

Quick Guide
  1. Commercial / consultancy tests: use the main submission form.
  2. Students & research cube tests: use the research portal.
  3. Always save your Application Number to track results.

Choosing the correct route ensures faster processing and proper record keeping.
