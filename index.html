Quick Guide

  1. Commercial: Click Submit Commercial Request and complete the form.
  2. Students/Research: Click Go to Research Portal for research cube tests.
  3. Save your Application Number after submission (it’s needed for tracking).
  4. Use Check Results to view the status/results with your Application Number.

Tip: If you can’t find your Application Number, check the PDF generated at submission or saved records.
