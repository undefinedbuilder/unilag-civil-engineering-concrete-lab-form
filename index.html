What the UNILAG Concrete Lab Does

We support industry and academic work through testing, design support, and practical capacity building.

  • Concrete Compressive Strength Test (cube strength verification)
  • Material Testing (cement, aggregates, and related checks)
  • Concrete Mix Design (mix proportioning and performance targets)
  • Training Services (hands-on lab training & best practices)
