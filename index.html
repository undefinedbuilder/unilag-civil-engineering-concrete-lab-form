Welcome. This portal helps you submit cube test/mix requests and check results using your application number.

About This Portal

The UNILAG Concrete Laboratory supports concrete testing and mix documentation for research and commercial engagements. Use the two options below to submit details or check outcomes.

Quick Guide

  1. Click Submit a Request to complete the form and generate your application number.
  2. Save your application number (e.g., UNILAG-CLR-000001).
  3. Click Check Results and enter your application number to view status/results.

Tip: If you can’t find your application number, check the PDF generated at submission or your saved records.
